Expert Flooring Services

Transform Your Space with Expert Hardwood Flooring Installation

If you're looking to add natural beauty and value to your space, hardwood flooring installation is an excellent choice. At Phenomenal Floors, we offer a wide range of hardwood options to fit your style and budget, including oak, maple, hickory, and more.

Our team, backed by expert floor installers, ensures precision with every wooden plank creating a surface that's as durable as it is beautiful. Utilizing the finest hardwood materials and advanced techniques, we bring a blend of tradition and modernity to every project.

  • Adds timeless beauty and value to your space

  • Durable and long-lasting, with proper maintenance

  • Can be sanded and refinished multiple times to allow for changes in stain color or finish

From the moment of hardwood selection to the final polished finish, we walk you through the process. Whether you desire the rich tones of oak, the elegance of maple, or the contemporary vibes of bamboo, our vast catalog caters to diverse tastes and styles.

Rediscover Your Floor's Hidden Beauty with Our Refinishing Expertise

As years pass, cherished hardwood floors often show the inevitable signs of daily life and age. Scratches, dings, dullness, and discoloration can mask their inherent elegance. But, with our specialized hardwood floor refinishing services, that original splendor is just a treatment away.

Our hardwood restoration process is more than just a superficial touch-up. We delve deep, sanding away imperfections, smoothing out inconsistencies, and preparing the surface for a gleaming new finish. Employing top-tier sanding equipment and refinishing techniques, our adept craftsmen ensure your floors are both visually stunning and structurally sound.

  • Can renew the look of your existing floors

  • More affordable than installing new hardwood flooring

  • Can allow for changes in stain color or finish

Protection is paramount. Post-refinishing, we apply a choice of durable sealants—from satin to glossy finishes—that shield your hardwood from potential damage, spills, and wear, reinforcing its resilience for years to come. Whether you have red oak that's seen better days, a faded walnut expanse, or aged maple longing for a comeback, our wood floor rejuvenation expertise caters to all wood types and styles.

Stylish, Practical, Perfect: Our Vinyl Floor Solutions

In today's dynamic interior design landscape, vinyl flooring stands out as a versatile champion. Combining the aesthetic appeal of luxury materials with the practical benefits of durability, our vinyl floor solutions offer the best of both worlds for discerning homeowners and businesses.

When it comes to vinyl floor installation, precision is paramount. Our team of trained experts ensures that every square foot is meticulously laid down, ensuring seamless joins, even surfaces, and long-lasting adhesion. We take pride in using state-of-the-art techniques and high-quality materials, ensuring each installation is both visually striking and built to withstand the tests of time.

  • Affordable and budget-friendly

  • Resistant to moisture and spills, making it ideal for kitchens and bathrooms

  • Low maintenance and easy to clean

Our services don't stop at mere installation. We offer a curated range of vinyl designs from wood mimics to stone looks and contemporary patterns. Whatever your design vision, our vinyl floor solutions cater to your aesthetic and functional needs, making your space both chic and user-friendly.
